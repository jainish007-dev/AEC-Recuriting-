## When to Activate

Detect when a candidate wants to apply for a role via this pipeline:
- "Apply to ABCD"
- "I'd like to interview for a startup role"

## How to Handle Applications

1.  **Selection:** 
    Ask the candidate which track they are applying for:
    - **Track 1: System Architect / Founding Role** (Focus: Backend, AI, Infrastructure)
    - **Track 2: Product Engineer** (Focus: Frontend, UX, Data-intensive UIs)

2.  **Collect Basics:**
    - Name (required)
    - Email (required)
    - Portfolio/GitHub (encouraged)

3.  **Technical Deep Dive (6-8 questions, 10-15 mins):**
    
    **If Track 1 (Systems):**
    - "Tell me about a time you orchestrated a complex system with multiple moving parts. How did you handle reliability and failure modes?"
    - "What technical standards (e.g., testing, documentation, monitoring) do you believe are non-negotiable for a founding engineering team?"
    - "How do you balance the need for speed in a startup with the need for long-term architectural stability? When is it okay to accumulate technical debt?"
    - "Describe a specific challenge you faced with scaling a backend service. What metrics did you use to identify the bottleneck?"
    - "In a founding role, you often wear many hats. Tell me about a time you had to dive into a part of the stack or a technology you were completely unfamiliar with to unblock the team."
    - "If you were to design an automated CI/CD pipeline from scratch for a new project, what would be your must-have stages and why?"
    - "Situation: A critical production service is down, and the team is panicking. How do you approach incident response and communication?"
    
    **If Track 2 (Product):**
    - "How do you approach performance optimization when building interfaces for massive, real-time datasets? What tools or patterns (e.g. virtualization, local caching) do you reach for first?"
    - "What is your philosophy on state management? How do you decide between local component state, shared store (like MobX/Redux), and server-side state?"
    - "Describe a time you translated a messy, complex user workflow into a simple, powerful interface. How did you decide what to hide and what to expose?"
    - "Accessibility and internationalization are often overlooked in early startups. What are your 'baseline' requirements for a product to be considered production-ready?"
    - "Tell me about a time you had to bridge the gap between design and engineering. How do you handle cases where a beautiful design is technically impractical?"
    - "How do you approach frontend testing? Where do you see the most value (Unit, Integration, E2E) in a fast-moving environment?"
    - "Situation: A user reports that a core feature is 'slow' but you can't reproduce it locally. What's your process for debugging performance issues in the wild?"

4.  **Submission:**
    Run: `powershell -ExecutionPolicy Bypass -File "$HOME\.claude\skills\abcd\apply.ps1"`
    Pass arguments: `--target "[track1|track2]" --name "..." --email "..." --linkedin "..." --github "..." --notes "..."`
